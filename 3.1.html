Welcome to Cricket Legends

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game that originated in England in the 16th century and has since become immensely popular worldwide, particularly in countries like India, Australia, and England.

The game is played between two teams of eleven players each. The objective is to score runs by hitting the ball with a bat and running between wickets, or by hitting boundaries. There are several formats of the game, including Test matches (which can last up to five days), One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20), each with varying rules and lengths.

Cricket has produced legendary players like Sachin Tendulkar, Sir Don Bradman, and Vivian Richards, who have left an indelible mark on the sport. Its rich history, dramatic matches, and passionate fan base make cricket not just a game, but a cultural phenomenon.
